The Internet of Everything is a concept in which the Internet connects everything in the world. It has allowed us to find new ways to work, entertain, and think. This idea doesn’t consist only of electronic devices; it also involves people and data. It can also include the process that the devices undergo to make better decisions. The way these are all connected by the internet will be different. For example, people can connect through social media platforms like Instagram or Facebook. They can also be connected through devices like mobile phones. In todays society, we see that our world is dependent on technology, and the way our lives function is reliant on these devices.
